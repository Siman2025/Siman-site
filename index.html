About Siman

Siman is a mobile massage service created by Vitty, a single mum who understands the pressures of balancing work, family, and self-care. With a foldable massage bed that sets up in minutes, Siman brings comfort and relaxation directly to your home.

Our focus is on women of all ages – from busy professionals and homemakers to older women seeking gentle care. Vitty takes pride in providing a dedicated, understanding, and restorative massage service tailored to women’s needs.
